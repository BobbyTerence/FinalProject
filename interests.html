Magic the Gathering

Magic the Gathering is a trading card game with multiple different playable formats, but the only format I play is Commander.

Commander is a 100 card singleton format. Singleton means that there can only be one copy of each unique card except for basic lands.

Commander also has a specific card called a Commander, which is a legendary creature in its own zone, which increases in cost each time it is cast from its zone.
